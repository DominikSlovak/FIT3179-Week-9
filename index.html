Repercussions of Deforestation

Forests are some of the richest areas for biodiversity on the globe. They provide a variety of habitats for plants, animals and micro-organisms. On average, a full grown tree will absorb more than 48 pounds of carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and release oxygen in exchange, and also plays a part in regulating global warming.
